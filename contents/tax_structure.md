# Tax Structure

1. Property taxes will serve as an alternative to rent to homes acquired by the Public Housing Trust. It shall be used to help fund irrigation and power supply. 
2. Most people fall under property tax, with exemptions mentioned in the SDR Constitution. 
3. Fuel tax is functionally the same as sales tax, but is channeled to road building an repair. This is not limited to gas stations, but also to charging stations. 
4. Income tax will be used to fund emergency services and healthcare. 
5. Sales tax will be used to fund education and technology development.
