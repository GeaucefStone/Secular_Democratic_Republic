## Civil War Under Constitutional Restoration

### Article XVI: The Legal Civil War

#### Constitutional Civil War Definition

A **Constitutional Civil War** is formally declared under Article XVI when:

 - A Systemic Constitutional Breach occurs (Section 2)

 - The 25% petition and ⅔ referendum thresholds are met (Section 3)  

 - The Provisional Council assumes authority against resisting government elements

 - **Regional Activation Option**: For crises affecting <50% of states, regional Provisional Councils may be established with proportional authority

#### Legal Status of Combatants

**Constitutional Forces**: Provisional Council, Defense Force loyalists, and citizen militias acting under Council authority

**Usurper Forces**: Government officials and military units refusing to recognize Article XVI activation

**Neutral Parties**: Civilians, humanitarian organizations, and international observers with protected status

**Legal Distinction**: Constitutional forces operate with full legal immunity; Usurper forces lose all legal protections

#### The 180-Day War Limitation with Extensions

Article XVI, Section 5 imposes strict temporal boundaries:

 - Conflict must conclude with new elections within six months

 - Single 90-day extension possible with ⅔ Citizens' Assembly approval

 - Mandatory international observation during any extension period

 - Prevents indefinite "emergency" governance
