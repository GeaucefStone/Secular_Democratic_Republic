# Construction Protocol: The Sentinel of Liberty

## Project Overview
Construction of a 30-foot hollow monumental statue using forged aluminum bronze-chromium alloy plates, assembled using advanced metallurgical techniques for maximum durability and symbolic resonance.

## Phase 1: Digital Design & Engineering

### 1.1 Digital Sculpting
- **Software Requirements:**
  - Blender 4.0+ (primary modeling)
  - ZBrush (detail refinement) 
  - Fusion 360 (engineering analysis)
  - ANSYS (structural simulation)

### 1.2 Structural Engineering Parameters
- **Statue Height:** 20 feet (6.096 meters)
- **Material Thickness:** 3/8 inch (9.525mm)
- **Internal Armature:** 316L Stainless Steel
- **Safety Factor:** 4.0 (4x expected load capacity)
- **Wind Load Design:** 150 mph maximum

### 1.3 Plate Segmentation Strategy
- Total plates: 87 individual components
- Maximum plate dimension: 4ft x 8ft (forge capacity limit)
- Lap joint design: 1.5-inch thickened edges
- Unique identifier engraved on interior of each plate
- Grain flow optimization for stress distribution

## Phase 2: Material Preparation

### 2.1 Alloy Specification
**Material Designation:** CuAl10Fe5Ni5-Cr

**Chemical Composition:**
- Copper: 78-82%
- Aluminum: 9-11% 
- Iron: 4-6%
- Nickel: 4-6%
- Chromium: 1.5-2.5%
- Manganese: 0.5% maximum

**Mechanical Properties:**
- Yield Strength: 450 MPa
- Tensile Strength: 750 MPa
- Hardness: 210 HB
- Elongation: 12% minimum
- Corrosion Resistance: Excellent (marine environment)

### 2.2 Plate Forging Process

#### 2.2.1 Blank Preparation
- Cut alloy sheets to rough dimensions +15% oversize
- Ultrasonic testing for material defects
- Chemical cleaning to remove surface contaminants

#### 2.2.2 Hot Forging Sequence
1. Heat treatment to 850°C (orange heat condition)
2. Power hammer forming to approximate shape
3. Maintain consistent grain flow direction
4. Intermediate annealing at 650°C if required

#### 2.2.3 Edge Cold Working
- Cool forged plates to room temperature
- Pneumatic hammer treatment of 2-inch border
- Target density increase: 15% at joint areas
- Hardness specification: Rockwell C35-40 at edges
- Microstructural verification required

## Phase 3: Welding & Assembly

### 3.1 Welding Specification

**Process:** Gas Tungsten Arc Welding (GTAW/TIG)

**Parameters:**
- Electrode: 2% Thoriated Tungsten, 3/32 inch
- Shielding Gas: Argon, 99.996% purity
- Gas Flow Rate: 20-25 CFH
- Filler Material: ERCuAl-A2
- Pre-heat Requirement: 200°C minimum
- Interpass Temperature: 150-200°C

### 3.2 Assembly Sequence

1. **Foundation Preparation**
   - Install reinforced concrete foundation
   - Mount internal armature framework
   - Verify anchor bolt alignment

2. **Primary Assembly**
   - Install central torso plates (sequence A1-A12)
   - Progress to limb sections (sequence B1-B24)
   - Attach head and facial components (sequence C1-C8)
   - Install robe drapery elements (sequence D1-D35)
   - Mount bow and arrow components (sequence E1-E8)

3. **Final Integration**
   - Verify all lap joint alignments
   - Conduct pre-weld fit-up inspection
   - Execute welding according to sequence

### 3.3 Quality Control Protocol

**Non-Destructive Testing Schedule:**
- Liquid penetrant testing after each weld pass
- Ultrasonic testing of completed seam sections
- Radiographic testing for critical structural joints
- Dimensional verification against CAD model

**Documentation Requirements:**
- Photographic record of each weld
- Inspector certification and signature
- Material traceability documentation
- Welding parameter logs

## Phase 4: Post-Weld Heat Treatment

### 4.1 PWHT Thermal Cycle

**Heat Treatment Protocol:**
- Phase 1: Ramp to 650°C at 100°C/hour
- Phase 2: Soak at 650°C for 4 hours
- Phase 3: Air cool to 300°C
- Phase 4: Furnace cool to room temperature

### 4.2 Treatment Facility Specifications

**Mobile Annealing Oven:**
- Temperature uniformity: ±10°C
- 64-point thermocouple monitoring array
- Real-time stress relief verification
- Emergency cooling system

**Quality Verification:**
- Hardness testing across HAZ regions
- Microstructural analysis samples
- Residual stress measurement
- Dimensional stability check

## Phase 5: Surface Finishing

### 5.1 Weld Smoothing Procedure

**Progressive Finishing Sequence:**
1. Angle grinder with 36-grit flap discs (rough shaping)
2. Progressive polishing through 80, 120, 240 grit
3. Final polishing to 800-grit mirror finish
4. Non-embedding compound for final buffing
