# LEGAL DICTIONARY OF THE SECULAR DEMOCRATIC REPUBLIC

## PREAMBLE

This dictionary serves as the reinforcement of this system's potential legal system. It is to help ensure appropriate sentencing, or no sentence at all. Some terms are created to have a more direct meaning. 

**Execution** - The lawful killing of a death row inmate or a war criminal

**Exoneration** - The act of lifting all relevant charges and releasing the inmate with new evidence, and requires financial award for time falsely spent. 

**Homicide** - The act of taking a life

**Manslaughter** - Unintentional homicide. Could be accidental, or in self-defense. 

**Murder** - Deliberate homicide

**Pardon** - The act of neutralizing existing charges as if they never happened. This does not erase their criminal record, but only restores full rights and removes the individual from prison. 

**Proxicide** - The act of using another person to commit murder. It could be a murderer for hire, a cult member, etc.
