### üö® Core Design Principles

The SDR Constitution is built upon five foundational pillars:

-   **The Anti-Corruption Engine:** A system where corruption is made legally and psychologically untenable through mechanisms like the "Poisoned Chalice" and stringent conspiracy laws.
-   **The Bodily Autonomy Absolute:** The self-ownership of one's body is the inviolable first law; no medical mandates are ever permitted.
-   **The Militant Defense of Liberty:** A democracy that proactively defends itself by disqualifying actors who seek to use its power to abolish fundamental rights.
-   **A Habitat for Dignity:** Guarantees of housing, healthcare, and a Universal Base Grant form the foundation for genuine human freedom and security.
-   **A Sovereign Digital Territory:** Rights to digital privacy, protection from synthetic media, and control over one's digital identity are enshrined as fundamental rights.
